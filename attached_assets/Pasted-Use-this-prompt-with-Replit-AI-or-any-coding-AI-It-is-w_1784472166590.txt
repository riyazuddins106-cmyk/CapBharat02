# Banner Module Redesign & Enhancement (UI/UX + Admin + Customer App)

You are acting as a **Principal UI/UX Designer, Senior Frontend Engineer, Senior Backend Engineer, Product Designer, and QA Engineer**.

Analyze the **Banner Section** in both the **Customer App** and **Admin Panel** and redesign it to production quality.

# QA Checklist

Verify

* Banner looks perfect on Android, iPhone, Tablet, and Desktop.
* Text never overlaps important parts of the image.
* CTA button is always visible and aligned.
* Images maintain quality without distortion.
* Admin can upload, edit, replace, crop, preview, reorder, schedule, activate/deactivate, and delete banners.
* All changes sync correctly between Admin Panel, Backend, and Customer App.
* Existing banners continue working after migration.
* No UI regressions or broken layouts remain.

**Goal:** Deliver a modern, premium, production-ready banner management system with a polished UI, flexible content controls, responsive behavior, and full end-to-end synchronization between the Admin Panel, Backend, and Customer App.
